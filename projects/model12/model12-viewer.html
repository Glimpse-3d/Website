MONA LISA

Leonardo da Vinci

Paris | France

The Mona Lisa, painted by Leonardo da Vinci in the early 16th century, is a renowned masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance.
Known for its captivating smile and mysterious expression, the portrait showcases da Vinci's mastery of technique, particularly in its use of sfumato.
Housed in the Louvre Museum, it is one of the most famous and celebrated works of art in the world.
